Rupam's Birthday celebration

28th June, 2021

Project : Hunger & Proverty

Event : Rupam's birthday

On 28th June 2021, on the occasion of Rupam's birthday we fed 30 helpless people at Ghutiari Sharif station adjacent areas. Initially it was Rupam's idea to provide meal to 15 people. After that Sunshine managed to provide meal to more 15 people. The food was totally made in home and packed with maintaining proper Covid guidelines.

The programme was successfully conducted with the co-operation of people over there and the with help of our members.

Thank you. Stay safe and healthy. Stay with us to provide little happiness to those people.



About Us

Our responsibility as fellow citizens in the midst of this misfortune of society is to stand by the helpless. Sunshine was born in constant obedience to this truth. We believe that our small initiatives can make a huge difference in society. Our main objective is to stand by the side of fellow citizens with honesty and devotion and to fight to the end for the progress of the society.
